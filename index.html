Meet Buplon

Buplon, an emissary from the planet Buplon, is here to bring Earth to a bright and beautiful future as our benevelont overlord. From cosmic healthcare reform to advanced peacekeeping initiatives, and an unending reign of terror, Buplon’s vision for the future is one of unity, prosperity, and universal access.

Platform & Policies

Nectar Extraction

To achieve lasting peace, Buplon proposes the strategic sucking of nectar from the brains of our enemies. Through interstellar treaties, we will extract and repurpose this energy for the benefit of Earth's citizens.

Galactic Economy

Opening trade and knowledge-sharing with advanced civilizations to create jobs and accelerate Earth’s technological progress.

Medicare for All

Buplon believes in universal healthcare. Buplon aims to create a healthcare system that’s out of this world.
